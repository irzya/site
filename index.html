    • IRYNA PRYTULA


    SAXOPHONIST


    Iryna Prytula is an alto-saxophonist. She performes solo, in duo "CHEEK TO CHEEK" and as session musician i different bands. Graduated from Lviv Music College and Lviv National Music Academy. Iryna participated in the Cho-Jazz International Jazz Summer Workshop in Poland.
